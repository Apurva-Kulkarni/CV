Honeysuckle Weekends

Independent Duo Band

Honeysuckle Weekends is a Pune based contemporary classical duo formed by producer Vaibhav Kulkarni and Composer, vocalists Apurva Kulkarni. The duo has released 6 Indian Classical fusion tracks till now under their project - 'Art That Remains' and has been recognized for its beautiful and modern age Indian sound.
